Welcome to the public shopping list!

    Please note any changes / additions you make here can also be altered by other users

    How to setup as an app

    iOS: Press share, then scroll down and press add to home screen

    NB. It won't show up in the App Library, however, it will show up if you pull down and search for it

    Android: Press the three dots in the top right corner (menu button), and press add to home screen

    How to use

    To add an item, type it in, and either press 'enter' on the keyboard or the 'add to cart' button to add to the list

    To delete an item, double tap it

    After you delete an item, it will be copied on your device, so if you accidentally delete or want to edit what's written, you have the option to paste it and re-enter it if you wish

    Please note that this app does require an internet connection - check below for how you can use it without

    About

    Please note this is a web-app; it is similar but is not the same as a native app like WhatsApp or Instagram, where you download the software on your device

    It is a website that acts and behaves as an app on your device when you add it to the home screen

    This means that it will behave as a regular app, that requires an internet connection, but it will take hardly any space on your device's storage

    On average, it takes around 1/1,000,000 of the storage that Instagram uses

    However, this does mean that if you share the URL of the website, anyone who has that link will be able to access your list

    No internet?

    If you are going to a shop where you know you will not have an internet connection, open the app in an area with connection beforehand, and leave it running in the background

    You will be able to view the list and also delete items locally, so you can tick off items as you shop, however when you reconnect to the internet and reload the app, the items will still be there as they cannot sync while the device is offline

    To remember which items you deleted, you can screenshot the current list before reloading the app, or use it as a checklist to go back through after your shop, and check you got everything you needed

    However, this is only in case you don't have service in a shop or they don't have Wi-Fi etc.
